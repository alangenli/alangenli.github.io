biography


Alan is a scientist living in the 14th arrondissement of Paris with interests in visual art, writing, and music.

He has contributed to the field of lithium-ion battery diagnostics by proposing pulse-based approaches to health, charge, and power estimation. He has also helped develop optical-fibre sensors within lithium and sodium-ion battery cells.

Beyond the world of batteries, Alan has interned at the Met Cloisters, and taken courses in art history and studio art.
